## Mastery Roadmap
The scripts in this repository are organized to show a gradual motion from pure mathematics to applied particle physics.

### Level 1: Mathematical Foundations
* **Estimating Pi & e:** Fundamental iterative methods to calculate universal constants.

### Level 2: Classical & Relativistic Physics
* **Lorentz Transformations:** Modeling time dilation and length contraction for high-velocity particles.
* **Exponential Decay:** Simulating radioactive half-life.

### Level 3: Advanced Thermal & Quantum Simulations
* **Metropolis Algorithm:** Simulating a 1D Ising Model to observe magnetic domain formation.
* **Blackbody Radiation:** Implementation of **Planck’s Law** to model spectral radiance.
* **Heisenberg Uncertainty:** Visualizing the relationship between position and momentum.
